# P0017
AI Powered X-Ray Scanning and Blockchain-Based Evidence Management for corruption Prevention.
AI-XBS (AI-Powered X-Ray & Blockchain System) is an advanced anti-corruption solution that integrates Artificial Intelligence, X-ray imaging, and blockchain technology to enhance security and transparency in inspection systems.

The system automates the detection of hidden or suspicious items using AI-based image analysis, reducing reliance on manual inspection, which is often slow and error-prone. Detected results are securely stored on a blockchain, ensuring tamper-proof records and preventing data manipulation.

By combining real-time detection, automated decision-making, and secure data management, AI-XBS improves efficiency, minimizes corruption risks, and strengthens accountability in areas such as airports, customs, and logistics.
